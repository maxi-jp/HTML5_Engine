Audio Manager

The AudioManager (from engine/audioplayer.js) provides a simple way to load and play sound effects and music in your game.

// In your game's Start method or a loading function
this.audioManager = new AudioManager();
this.audioManager.loadSound('jumpSound', 'assets/sounds/jump.wav');

// To play a sound
this.audioManager.playSound('jumpSound');

// To play music (loops by default)
this.audioManager.playMusic('backgroundMusic', 'assets/music/bg_music.mp3');
